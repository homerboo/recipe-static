Pressure-Cooked Garlic Confit

Yield: 300 grams

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 120 mins

Total Time: 135 mins

Special Equipment: Pressure Cooker, Canning Jar

Difficulty: Medium

Shopping List

  • Bunch of garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Rosemary (optional, 2g)
  • Thyme (optional, 2g)

Preparation

No prep bowls
  1. Slice the weird thing off the bottom of the garlic clove.
  2. Gently, using the flat end of a meat tenderizing hammer on the garlic with a light palm, tapping the clove should separate the garlic from the paper somewhat easily. Also, wet hands always help with garlic paper.

Directions

  1. Place all in a canning jar, ensuring oil covers garlic, tighten lid fully, then unscrew one-quarter turn so the jar doesnâ€™t explode (this should be done with fingers)
  2. Place the filled jar on a trivet (rack) in the base of a pressure cooker and add 2.5cm of water
  3. Pressure cook on high for two hours
  4. Let the cooker cool naturally
  5. Let the jar of garlic cool naturally
  6. Serve warm or cool

Notes

Keeps refrigerated for 1 month

Can also do this with ginger but I'd use 1 inch cubes
