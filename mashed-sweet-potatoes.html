Yield: 6-8 portions

Prep Time: 5

Cook: 15 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Special Equipment:

Difficulty: Easy

Shopping List

  • 6 medium sweet potatoes
  • Stick of butter
  • Heavy cream (18% is fine)
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Sage
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground pepper

Preparation

3 prep bowls
  1. Peeled potatoes sliced into quarters and halved
  2. Melted butter with sage
  3. 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon, 1/2 tsp ground pepper, pinch of ground cloves and nutmeg

Directions

  1. Bring pot of salted water to a boil over medium heat
  2. Add the sweet potatoes and lower heat to a simmer
  3. Simmer for 15 mins or until potatoes pierce easily with a fork
  4. Drain sweet potatoes and return to pot
  5. Add sage butter and cream along with spices
  6. Mash lightly unless you like potato glue
